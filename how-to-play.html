Frequently Asked Questions

How do I play-to-earn cash?

Use your daily games energy allotment that comes with your Player Level to enter any of the Daily Fantasy Stock and Crypto cash prize games. These prize pools are created by vig and our sponsors for you to be able to play to earn!

How do I climb the skill leaderboard and earn even more cash?

Skill leaderboard rankings are based on vig’s propriety formula which is a combo of placings, wins, and prize pool earnings. This leaderboard pays out prizes to top earners on a weekly and monthly basis.

How do I acquire coins to enter play-to-earn Coin Games?

You can acquire coins either by purchasing coin packs OR by completing coin reward tasks that are always available for you in the Rewards section of the app.

How do I climb the coin leaderboard to earn even more coins?

Coin leaderboard rankings are based on aggregate total of coins earned. This leaderboard pays out prizes to top earners on a weekly and monthly basis.

How can I spend my Coins?

You can spend coins to enter one of our daily coin prize games. Finish in-the-money to win even more coins and climb the weekly and monthly coin leaderboards for additional pay out opportunities.

Coins can also be spent on Daily Player Level 1/2/3 Passes that give you access to additional tools and features and increased energy to enter more cash prize games.
